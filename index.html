Trunk or Treat at Marlboro High School is an exciting, family-friendly Halloween event where participants decorate the trunks of their cars with spooky, fun, or creative themes and distribute candy to children. It provides a safe, controlled environment for trick-or-treating, especially for younger kids, and encourages community involvement. Each decorated car (or "trunk") is parked in a designated area, typically in the school's parking lot, and children walk from car to car, collecting treats just as they would from house to house. The event often includes additional activities like costume contests, games, and sometimes food stands, making it a full Halloween experience for families. The Marlboro High School Trunk or Treat emphasizes safety, creativity, and fun, allowing both parents and children to enjoy the Halloween spirit in a community setting.
