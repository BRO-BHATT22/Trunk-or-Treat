About the Event

Our annual Trunk or Treat event is the perfect way to celebrate Halloween. Bring your family, decorate your trunks, and enjoy games, candy, and spooky fun!

This year's event will feature exciting activities for children and adults, including a trunk decorating contest, costume parade, and much more!
